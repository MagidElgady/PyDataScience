Seaborn is a data visualization library based on matplotlib and is used to create
statistical graphics.
. Can compare multiple variables
. Supports multi-plot grids
. Plots linear regression automatically
. Has multiple colour palettes available
. Allows for bivariate and univariate visualizations
. Makes it easier to improve the look of a graph
Note: Seaborn requires the following libraries: pandas, numpy, scipy, statsmodels and matplotlib.
